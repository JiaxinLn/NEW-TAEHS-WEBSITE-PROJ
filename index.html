About Us

Thomas A. Edison CTE High School was founded in 1950 and named after the famous American inventor and businessman Thomas Edison. Edison wants students, teachers, parents, staff, and everyone else to feel safe and in a place where their ideas can be heard.

Our mission is to develop leaders of tomorrow by preparing all students to meet the high academic, technical, civic, and workforce challenges of the twenty-first century. We believe in academic rigor, high standards for assessing students' performances, and continuous school improvement.

Our core values include not leaving people behind and moving as one. We believe in motivating our students by helping them discover their passions and excel in their chosen fields.

At Edison, our personality is a blend of being driven, serious, and fun. We prioritize completing tasks while creating an environment where students can also enjoy their learning journey.

Academics

Here at Edison, we offer a wide range of academic programs to cater to diverse interests and career paths. Our courses include engineering courses such as Electrical Installation, Automotive and Autobody, Robotics, Architectural Drafting, IT: Cybersecurity, IT Networking, IT Specialist, and many more.

Our goal is to provide students with a hands-on learning experience that prepares them for real-world challenges. We also offer AP classes like AP Calculus, AP Literature and Composition, AP US Government and Politics, AP Chemistry, and AP Statistics to challenge and stimulate our students.
